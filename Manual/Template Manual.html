On the Subject of 9000

What does the power scouter say about his power level?

You have been given a powerful opponent, and in order to take them down, you must submit the right combination of sounds to launch your attack.

To aid you in finding the right combination, a total of four pictures out of five symbols will be presented on the module.
- For every Turtle present, select "KA - ME"
- For every Staff present, select "HA - ME"
- For every Tail present, select "HA"
- If Stars are present, repeat the selections 2 times for every set of stars

The Z clause


If there is a Z present, the order of the selections will be altered from the original sequence. The original sequence goes as follows: Turtle selections and Staff selections alternate, followed by any Tail selections, then repeat the sequence for the number of Stars present.
The changes to the sequence will be as follows, based on the first rule that applies:
- If the Turtle is present in the top left, replace all staff selections with ME - KA
- If the Turtle is present in the top right, change any second sound in a selection so that it matches the first. [HA - ME becomes HA - HA]
- If the Staff is present in the bottom left, replace all turtle selections with ME - HA
- If the Tail is present in the bottom right, reverse the entire sequence.
- If only Stars are present, enter sequence: HA - ME - HA - ME - KA

Four of a Kind


There is a small chance that all of the symbols will be the same. Enter the solution as follows, ignoring all above rules:
-Turtle/Staff: KA - ME
-Tail: KA - ME - HA
-Star: KA - ME - HA - ME - HA
-Z: Enter whatever you want! As soon as your sequence is complete, the module will be solved.
